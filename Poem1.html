🌌 Cosmic Pull

You are a Sagittarius A in my Milky Way, A tesseract in my three-dimensional world. You are the moon in my night, The sun in my system, The universe in my telescope. I am bound by you, I can feel you. I am drawn to you, I orbit around you. Pulled by your gravity, My heart will feel never reaching you. Bound by rules, Trapped in time, Limited by space, Blurred by reality A silent observer from afar, Forever quantumly entangled.

- Surendhar
